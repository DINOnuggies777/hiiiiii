DIFFERENT TYPES OF ANIMALS BASED ON THIER FOOD CHAIN

There are over 1.5 million species of animals on our planet, but they eat different things based on what group thier eating catigories and habitat.

There are many types of vores and they are;

  • Carnivore: the eating of animals.
  • Herbivore: the eating of plants
  • Algivore: eating algae (both macroalgae and microalgae)
  • Omnivore: the eating of both plants, animals, fungi, bacteria etc.
  • Fungivore: the eating of fungus.
  • Bacterivore: the eating of bacteria
